Ramallah

Ramoun is a Palestinian town , located to the northeast of the city of Ramallah and part of the Ramallah and Al-Bireh Governorate . It is 15 km away from it, rising 780 m above sea level. It is connected by an internal road connecting it to the main road, 1.4 km long. The Romans called it “Ramoun” and the same in Phoenician , Aramaic and Syriac . Its plural is “Rumana” is the name of a common Semitic god, the god of storm and thunder .
